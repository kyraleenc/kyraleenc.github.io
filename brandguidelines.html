Brand Guidelines

"KINKA IZAKAYA Brand Guidelines" is a Capstone project for school that I have created using Snagit, Canva, and Adobe Illustrator. The purpose of this project is to create a style guide for an existing company to enforce a common and consistent look and feel within and across documents.

❮ ❯

Click here to view the full brand guidelines.

Challenge

Develop brand guidelines for KINKA IZAKAYA (a Japanese restaurant in Canada) both in terms of visuals and text.

User Analysis

This guide aims to promote a sense of brand identity in all external and internal communications. The audiences of this guide would be internal employees maintaining KINKA IZAKAYA's website, documents (i.e. menus), and promotional materials.

Here are some things they might be looking for from this guide:

  • How they should scale and position the logo
  • What tone they should adopt when creating materials for KINKA IZAKAYA
  • Instructions on grammar, style and terminology
  • What legal elements to include in company documentations
