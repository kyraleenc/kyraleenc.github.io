Who am I?

Hello! My name is Kyra Lee and I am a Technical Writer in Toronto. When I was a kid, my dream was to speak 100 languages so I could communicate with everyone. Now, that's exactly what I do at work. Well... It's close enough.

As a technical writer, I develop documentation that breaks down complex technical information into clear and digestible pieces for users. I have experience in creating technical training materials, software demo videos, and end-user documentation. I frequently work with clients in the software, hardware, aerospace, and other technical industries. I also work as a project coordinator to ensure projects run smoothly and are completed on time and within budget.

I attended Queen's Univerity for my undergrad and received a degree in Language, Literature, and Cultural Studies, with a minor in Global Development Studies. Before discovering Technical Communication, I did internships in multiple fields, including marketing, public relations, intercultural communications, and law.

In my free time, I like to research and develop new skills. I believe that learning and perpetual and am always trying to keep myself busy! I still love learning languages, and I currently speak 5 (I know... still a long way to go to achieve my childhood dream). I rescued three lovely kittens when they were abandoned at my house, so I also spend a good portion of my time cuddling with them.
